## Clone Wars: Unbound

**Clone Wars: Unbound** is a large-scale, player-driven Clone Wars experience where every action leaves a lasting impact on the galaxy.

The project features permanent character death, progression based on reputation and service, and massive 200+ player operations across planets and space.

Players fight alongside or against AI-controlled armies and iconic lore characters while contributing to a connected galactic economy where ships, weapons, and resources are finite.

From frontline troopers to powerful Jedi, every player can influence the outcome of the war through strategy, logistics, and battlefield decisions.
